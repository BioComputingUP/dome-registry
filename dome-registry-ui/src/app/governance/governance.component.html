DOME Governance

The governance structure for DOME (Recommendations and Registry) ensures strategic direction, alignment with community needs, and operational sustainability. Its development and priorities are guided by a formal Scientific Advisory Board and continuous input from key international groups within ELIXIR, while also actively incorporating feedback from scientific publishers, industry representatives, Machine Learning specialists, and data infrastructure experts to ensure broad relevance and effectiveness.

Scientific Advisory Board (SAB)

The DOME SAB consists of internationally recognized external experts who provide independent strategic advice and critical evaluation. The SAB helps ensure that DOME remains aligned with the evolving needs of these diverse communities and adheres to best practices in scientific rigor, Machine Learning and Data Stewardship.

ELIXIR AI Ecosystem Focus Group

Role: The ELIXIR AI Ecosystem (AIE) Focus Group is a primary source of scientific AI/ML expertise and community feedback. DOME originated from this group's prior efforts in the ELIXIR Machine Learning Focus Group to address ML reporting challenges in life sciences.

Influence on DOME: The AIE Focus Group provides crucial input on the practical needs of researchers applying AI/ML, ensuring the DOME Recommendations and Registry features remain relevant to scientific practice. Their activities directly inform DOME's technical and conceptual development based on real-world use cases.

ELIXIR Data Platform

Role: The ELIXIR Data Platform provides strategic context and guidance related to data infrastructure best practices within ELIXIR, focusing on FAIR principles, sustainability, and interoperability.

Influence on DOME: DOME aligns with the Platform's goals for FAIR data resources. Input from the Platform helps ensure the DOME Registry adheres to robust technical standards for databases, uses recommended interoperability solutions (like Bioschemas, Identifiers.org), and follows best practices for sustainable resource management, benefiting from ELIXIR-wide infrastructure expertise.
